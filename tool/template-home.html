Interledger

Interledger is an open protocol suite for sending payments across different ledgers.

Like routers on the Internet, connectors route packets of money across independent payment networks. The open architecture and minimal protocol enable interoperability for any value transfer system.

Interledger is not tied to any one company, blockchain, or currency.
